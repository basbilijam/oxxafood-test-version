What we do

Oxxafood’s online platform directly connects professional buyers with growers of organic fruit and vegetables, and organizes the sales, purchasing and logistics for both parties.

No intermediaries

The platform cuts out intermediaries that add high margins.

Direct from grower to buyer

Buyers have access to organic products at cost-price.

Affordable prices

It will be much more affordable for consumers to buy organic food!

Organic produce

The consumption of organic fruit and vegetables has grown exponentially in the past decade. More and more people consciously choose products grown without chemical pesticides and synthetic fertilizers, and thereby contribute to a more sustainable world.

Affordable for everyone!

Unfortunately, the high prices of organic products mean that they are only available to a small part of the population. Oxxafood was borne out of the desire to change this situation, and has set itself the mission to make organic fruit and vegetables available and affordable to everyone!

Contact

Oxxafood is a wholesaler of organic fruit and vegetables based in Veenendaal (Netherlands) and Loriol (France). We deliver products in a contemporary and modern way, from our suppliers to professional customers throughout Europe and beyond.
